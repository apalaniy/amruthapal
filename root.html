Reflections

As we developed our platform, Root Health, we were able to discover the sorts of needs the market still hasn’t addressed with regards to nutrition. The idea of an affordable on call nutritionist is something everyone we spoke to thought was a unique idea and something they themselves would use given an affordable price. Our platform seeks to meet a very unique need in the market and challenges the traditional sorts of interactions between nutritionists and their patients. Furthermore, as for the design process itself, we took initiative and reached out and received feedback from not just professors of the class, but coffee goers at starbucks, shopping moms at Giant Eagle, and nutritionists themselves. We iterated several times to develop the perfect combination of color, design, and usability to deliver to our consumers and providers.

 

Next steps include conducting usability testing with our prototype, further iterations, and further feature development.
