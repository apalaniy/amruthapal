Overview

Our team was tasked with proposing a service innovation to Sheetz, a multimillion dollar gas and convenience store chain. Our solution, My Sheetz Pass, is an RFID tag integrated loyalty pass, placed on the windshield of a car, that gives customers the option for automatic payments, order ahead, and in-car delivery.

Solution

We embraced a fail fast philosophy and prototyped & tested many solutions to find the ideal service innovation that could push Sheetz into the next generation of gas station and convenience store service. A complete look into our ideation and iteration processes can be found here. Our efforts culminated in My Sheetz Pass, a rebundling of Sheetz' current loyalty program using RFID technology.

 

The Pitch | The Customer Experience

We presented our idea to Emily Sheetz and other executives at Sheetz' new innovation hub in Baker Square, Pittsburgh. To help our audience visualize the depth and scope of our pitch, we used a persona, customer journey map, lo fi movie prototype and paper prototype.
